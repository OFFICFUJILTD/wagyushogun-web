Self-style Yakiniku

Your Wagyu,
Your Grill.

Yakiniku is a ritual, not a performance. At WAGYU SHOGUN, your A5 Wagyu arrives fresh and you control the grill — your timing, your temperature, your experience. Intimate by design: we serve just 15–20 portions per session.

Reserve Your Table
01

Arrive & Be Seated

Your personal table grill is ready. No rush, no noise — just the quiet anticipation of what's coming. Your server walks you through everything.

02

Select Your Cut

A5 Kagoshima Kuroge, Iwate Iwachiku, or the classic Sukiyaki — each sourced direct from JA-certified farms in Japan.

03

Grill at Your Table

Your Wagyu arrives fresh, prepared for the grill. Cook to your preferred doneness — the act of grilling is inseparable from the pleasure of eating.

04

Savour Every Bite

Unlimited miso, rice, yuzu kosho, house BBQ sauce, Wagyu curry, seasonal dessert. Unhurried, unforgettable. As it should be.
