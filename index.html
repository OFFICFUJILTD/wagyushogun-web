Self-style Yakiniku

Your Wagyu,
Your Grill.

Yakiniku is a ritual, not a performance. At WAGYU SHOGUN, your A5 Wagyu arrives fresh and you control the grill — your timing, your temperature, your experience. Limited to 15–20 sets per session. Once sold out, service closes for the day.
