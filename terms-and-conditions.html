02 — Reservation Policy

Reservations & Bookings

All reservations at WAGYU SHOGUN are accepted exclusively via our online booking system. We do not accept reservations by phone. A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of a confirmation message from us.

  • Reservations must be made online at wagyushogun.com/reservation.
  • Please arrive on time. Late arrivals may result in a reduced dining time or forfeiture of your table.
  • Table holds are limited to 15 minutes past the reserved time, after which the reservation may be released.
  • Requests for specific seating preferences are noted but cannot be guaranteed.

Cancellation requirement: All cancellations must be submitted more than 24 hours before your scheduled reservation time. Cancellations within 24 hours are considered late cancellations and may be subject to the no-show policy below.

03 — No-Show Policy

No-Shows

Due to our strictly limited capacity of 15–20 portions per session, a no-show has a direct and significant impact on our operations and on other guests who were unable to secure a table.

A no-show is defined as a failure to arrive for a confirmed reservation without prior cancellation submitted more than 24 hours in advance. In the event of a no-show:

  • The reservation is considered forfeited with no refund or rescheduling entitlement.
  • Any pre-paid amounts or vouchers applied to the booking may be voided.
  • WAGYU SHOGUN reserves the right to decline future reservations at its discretion.

If you are unable to attend, please cancel as early as possible via the confirmation email you received at the time of booking.

04 — Capacity & Session Limits

Strictly 15–20 Portions Per Session

WAGYU SHOGUN operates on an intentionally limited capacity model. Each dining session accommodates a maximum of 15 to 20 portions only. This is a fundamental part of our commitment to quality, attentiveness, and the authentic Yakiniku experience.

As a result, walk-in guests cannot be accommodated when sessions are fully booked, and we strongly encourage advance reservations. We cannot guarantee availability for unbooked guests, regardless of group size or prior visit history.

Session times are fixed. Dining beyond the scheduled session end time is not available. We appreciate your understanding that these boundaries exist to protect the experience for all guests.
