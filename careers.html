Careers at
Wagyu Shogun

From everyday New Zealand to the world — authentic Japanese Wagyu. We're looking for people who share that conviction.

Why Work Here

Small Team.
High Standards.
Real Growth.

WAGYU SHOGUN is a boutique operation by design — we serve just 15–20 portions per session, and every team member matters. We need people who take independent responsibility seriously, bring genuine hospitality to every interaction, and want to be part of something that doesn't cut corners.

In return: competitive pay with a clear progression structure, a genuine understanding of Japan's finest beef culture, and long-term opportunities including visa support for the right candidates.

Front of House — Service

Now Hiring Part-time Royal Oak

You're the face of the WAGYU SHOGUN experience — guiding guests through the Yakiniku ritual, keeping the floor moving with quiet confidence, and ensuring every visit is seamless. We train you thoroughly on Wagyu and the self-style BBQ experience. What we need from day one: warm hospitality instincts, strong English, and a proactive, positive mindset.

Schedule 2–3 days/week, lunch & dinner shifts
Hours ~16–24 hours per week
Starting Pay $25 NZD / hour
Progression $25 → $28.50 → $30

Full-time — Floor Lead / Operations

Now Hiring Full-time Leadership Path

A full-time role for someone ready to step into a senior position. You'll support day-to-day operations, help train new team members, and hold the standard across both lunch and dinner services. Ideal candidates bring restaurant management or senior service experience, and the kind of "self-motivated, responsible personality" that thrives in a high-quality boutique environment.

Social media and operational task capability is a strong advantage — we're a small team and everyone contributes across the business.

Hours 40–45 hours/week
Schedule Flexible roster, both services
Pay Range $25–$30 NZD / hour
Visa Support Available for right candidate
