Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026  ·  NZ Privacy Act 2020

At WAGYU SHOGUN, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

01 Introduction

WAGYU SHOGUN (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates as a restaurant business located at 726 Manukau Road, Royal Oak, Auckland 1023, New Zealand. We offer dining experiences, online reservations, and related services through our website at www.wagyushogun.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected by WAGYU SHOGUN through our website, reservation systems, in-restaurant operations, and any other channels through which you interact with us. By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

We comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and its Information Privacy Principles (IPPs). If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your information, please contact us using the details at the end of this document.

02 Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information when you make a reservation, contact us, or use our website:

  • Full name — to identify you and personalise your dining experience.
  • Phone number — for reservation confirmation and contact purposes.
  • Postal address — where relevant to your interactions with us.
  • Email address — for reservation confirmations, communications, and marketing (where you have opted in).
  • Date of birth — to recognise special occasions and offer birthday benefits where applicable.
  • Gender — to help us tailor our communications where you choose to provide it.
  • Dietary requirements and allergy information — to ensure your safety and to accommodate your needs during your visit. This may include sensitive health information and is handled with the utmost care.
  • Reservation details — including date, time, party size, and any special requests.
  • UTM tracking data — information about how you arrived at our website (e.g. via a marketing campaign, social media, or search engine), collected automatically through URL parameters.

We only collect personal information that is necessary for our business functions and activities. You are not required to provide all information requested, but some services — such as reservations — may not be available if essential details are not provided.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Reservation management — processing and confirming table bookings, managing waitlists, and following up on reservations.
  • Customer service — responding to enquiries, resolving issues, and providing assistance before, during, and after your visit.
  • Safety and dietary needs — ensuring that allergy and dietary information is communicated to our kitchen team to keep you safe.
  • Special occasion recognition — using date of birth information to acknowledge birthdays and other milestones.
  • Marketing communications — sending you news, special offers, and updates about WAGYU SHOGUN, where you have given your consent to receive such communications.
  • Service improvement — analysing usage patterns and customer feedback to improve our services, website, and overall dining experience.
  • Legal obligations — complying with applicable New Zealand laws and regulations.

We will not use your personal information for any purpose that is incompatible with the reason it was originally collected, without first seeking your consent.

04 Marketing Communications

From time to time, we may invite you to join our mailing list to receive updates, seasonal menus, special offers, and exclusive dining events at WAGYU SHOGUN. Subscribing to our mailing list is entirely optional and separate from your reservation or dining activity.

When you subscribe to our mailing list — either through our website, in-restaurant sign-up, or by opting in during the reservation process — we will use your email address and name to send you relevant communications. We aim to make every communication valuable and relevant to your interests.

You can opt out at any time. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom of the message. Clicking this link will immediately remove you from our mailing list, and you will receive no further marketing communications from us. Alternatively, you may contact us directly at hello@wagyushogun.com to request removal from our list.

Please note that operational communications — such as reservation confirmations or important notices relating to your booking — are not marketing communications and will still be sent even if you unsubscribe from marketing.

05 Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Secure storage of data on reputable, industry-standard platforms with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Limiting access to personal information to staff members who need it to perform their job functions.
  • Regular review of our data security practices.
  • Using secure connections (HTTPS) on our website.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for the purposes described in this policy and will not be shared with, sold to, or traded with any other business, organisation, or individual for commercial gain.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

06 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses limited tracking technologies to improve your experience and to help us understand how visitors use our site.

Google Maps — Our website embeds Google Maps to display our restaurant location. When you view a page containing a Google Maps embed, Google may set cookies on your device and collect information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control the cookies set by Google Maps.

UTM Tracking Parameters — When you visit our website via a link from a marketing campaign, social media post, or email, the URL may contain UTM (Urchin Tracking Module) parameters (e.g., utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign). We use this data to understand which of our marketing efforts are most effective, so we can focus on providing you with the most relevant content and offers. This data does not identify you personally.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies; however, doing so may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

07 Third Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to help us operate our business. These include:

  • Reservation and booking platforms — we may use third-party reservation management software to process your booking. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and contractual obligations to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable law.
  • Payment processing — if payment is collected online or in-restaurant via a third-party payment processor, that processor is responsible for the security of payment card information. We do not store credit or debit card details on our systems.
  • Google Maps — embedded mapping service provided by Google LLC. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles data collected through Maps embeds.
  • Email marketing platforms — if you subscribe to our mailing list, your email address and name may be stored and managed on a third-party email marketing platform. These platforms operate under their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

We only share your information with third parties to the extent necessary to provide you with our services. We do not authorise third parties to use your personal information for their own marketing purposes.

08 Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the following rights in relation to your personal information held by WAGYU SHOGUN:

  • Right of access — you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will respond to your request within 20 working days.
  • Right of correction — if you believe that any personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date, you have the right to request that we correct it.
  • Right to request deletion — you may request that we delete your personal information where it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, subject to any legal obligations requiring retention.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where our processing is based on your consent (such as marketing emails), you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to make a complaint — if you believe we have breached the Privacy Act 2020 or handled your personal information in a way that is inconsistent with this policy, you may contact us directly or lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at www.privacy.org.nz.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in the Contact Us section below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

09 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

  • Reservation data — information related to your bookings (including name, contact details, dietary requirements, and party size) is retained for a reasonable period following your visit to support customer service, resolve any disputes, and comply with our legal and accounting obligations. This is generally up to seven years in line with New Zealand tax and business record-keeping requirements.
  • Marketing data — your email address and name on our mailing list will be retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Allergy and health information — sensitive dietary and allergy information collected at the time of your reservation is retained only for as long as it is relevant to your dining history with us.

When personal information is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymise it in accordance with our data management practices.
